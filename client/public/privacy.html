Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Kristy is a nutrition assistant you talk to in a chat. To do that well, Kristy keeps track of what you tell it. This page explains, in plain language, what we collect, why, and what we do — and don't do — with it.

What we collect

We only collect what's needed to run the service:

  • Your phone number — to create and sign in to your account via a one-time SMS code.
  • Weight entries — the weigh-ins you log, so Kristy can track your trend over time.
  • Meal logs — the foods you record (typed, photographed, or scanned) and their macros.
  • Chat messages — your conversation with Kristy, so it can respond in context and you can reload past days.
  • Your goals and profile — details you provide during onboarding (such as your goal, training, and eating pattern) used to personalize guidance.

Why we collect it

This information exists for one purpose: to provide the personalized nutrition service. Kristy uses your history, goals, and trends to give specific, relevant guidance instead of generic advice. We do not use your data to build advertising profiles.

Where it's stored

Your data is stored using Supabase, a third-party database and authentication provider, on our behalf. Your meal logs, chat messages, and other content are scoped to your account. To generate responses, the content of your messages (and food photos you send) is processed by our AI provider to produce Kristy's replies.

We don't sell your data

We do not sell your personal data to third parties, and we don't share it with third parties except the service providers we rely on to operate Kristy (such as our database, hosting, and AI providers described above), or where required by law.

Deleting your data

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. For now, email us at hello@kristy.app and we'll remove it. We'll build a one-tap deletion control into the app as it matures.

Kristy is not a medical provider

Kristy provides general nutrition guidance and is not a licensed dietitian, doctor, or medical provider. It does not diagnose, treat, or provide medical advice. The information Kristy gives is for general informational purposes only. Talk to a qualified healthcare professional before making significant changes to your diet, especially if you have any health condition or concern.

Changes to this policy

Kristy is in early access and evolving. If we make meaningful changes to how we handle your data, we'll update this page and revise the date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@kristy.app.
